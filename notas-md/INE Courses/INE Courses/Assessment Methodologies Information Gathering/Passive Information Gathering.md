# **Passive Information Gathering**

Passive information gathering refers to the collection of data or information without directly interacting with the target or source of that information. It involves gathering information passively, often through methods such as monitoring, surveillance, data analysis, or observing publicly available information.

- whois: a query and response protocol that is used for querying databases that store an Internet resource's registered users or assignees
